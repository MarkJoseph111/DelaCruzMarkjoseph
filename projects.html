The Forest is an immersive text-based RPG where you awaken in a mysterious forest with no memory of who you are or how you arrived. As you explore your surroundings, you stumble upon a tribe's camp where your journey unfolds. Interact with the tribe, learn their ways, and uncover the secrets of the forest as you navigate through a branching narrative filled with choices that shape your destiny.
